THE AFTER SCHOOL PROGRAM JEWISH PARENTS HAVE BEEN WAITING FOR

AT JBRO, YOUR SON WILL BE PROUD TO BE JEWISH…AND SO WILL YOU

  1. Connect your middle school aged son to the beauty of his Jewish heritage in a fun, substantive, and constructive way
  2. An opportunity for your son to learn about his heritage and make significant accomplishments in the study of Torah
  3. A talented and dedicated tutor handpicked for your son to study from a Torah text
  4. A chance for your son to connect with other Jewish boys from the community
  5. Lecture and refreshments for you while your children study and have fun
  6. Weekly JBro event: A half-hour of tutoring and an hour of sports
  7. You will be kept closely apprised of your son's accomplishments
  8. Everything is free of charge

After meeting with each family we pair up middle school aged boys with talented and dedicated young men who will learn Torah subjects with the boys. Each pair, boy and mentor – together called a "chavrusa", will learn according to the student's own level and interests, aiming for real and quantifiable accomplishments – one chavrusa may strive to complete half a page of Prophets per week, while another may attempt to learn 2 mishnas per week. All of JBro's mentors are young men personally known by Meir and Tzvi to have exemplary character and vast knowledge of Judaism and the Torah. The mentors serve as positive role models as well as educators for the boys at JBro. Parents are kept apprised of their son's learning and progress at all times and are invited to ask questions of their own.

Our goal is to give each boy an appreciation for the beauty of his heritage as well as a sense of pride in his Jewishness. At JBro your son will learn what it means to be Jewish. There is a weekly meeting on Sunday evenings at the JCC where the boys study with their mentor for a half hour. During this time parents are invited to attend an optional lecture. After learning with their mentors, the boys participate in sports activities headed by Ben Bashari.

At JBro we believe that in order to teach Torah we must meet each individual student at his own level, work with him according to his spiritual needs, and help him achieve at his own pace.

We are excited to meet you and your son and find him the perfect Jewish Brother!
